EVANGELISM DEPARTMENT

HISTORICAL CONTEXT: Evangelism Department of the Church of God (M&A) is one of the departments of the Pastors’ Fellowship, Church of God, Meghalaya and Assam was formed on the 17th June, 2003. It is the department that carried out the ministry of the Church to promote the teaching of Jesus Christ, his life, death and resurrection. The concept of evangelism is deeply rooted in the New Testament, where Jesus and his disciples preached the gospel and make disciples. Evangelism, in a Christian perspective, refers to the practice of sharing the Christian message or the love of God for humanity (Jn.3:16). It is essentially about proclaiming the “good news” of Jesus Christ and inviting people to have a personal relationship with him. The term evangelism is derives from the Greek word “euangelion” meaning “good news” or “gospel” or in other words it refers to the propagation of the life and teaching of Jesus Christ to all people.

METHODS & STRATEGIES

Evangelism is usually adopted through certain methods and strategies to reach out the gospel to others and some of them can be mentioned as under:

  • It usually conducted mass evangelism once a year in a place where permission is available.
  • Visitation is done in a place where people invite the department at their interest for private purposes.
  • Personal evangelism is open for common good.
  • Prayers and Counselling.
  • Bible Studies
  • Visitation to Local Churches
