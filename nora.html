NORA EVALYN HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL

A BRIEF HISTORY ABOUT THE SCHOOL

The establishment of the Nora Evalyn Higher Secondary School (English Medium) under the auspices of the Business Association of the Church Of God (Meghalaya & Assam), was in sweet and loving memory of (L) Mrs. N.E. Nichols-Roy, wife of (L) Rev. J.J.M. Nichols-Roy who rendered her life for the sake of the proclamation of the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ through the Church Of God in the Khasi & Jaintia Hills founded in 1992.

NORA EVALYN HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL
AIMS & OBJECTIVES OF THE SCHOOL
  • To educate the young pupils in all spheres of life so as to make them worthy, god fearing and discipline citizens of the country.
  • To cultivate in the mind of the young pupils the attitude of good will, sincerity and sense of hard work. To instill in them confidence and ability to achieve, to develop obedience to teachers and elders and to attain all desired attributes of good citizenship in the society and towards the country as a whole.
  • To provide opportunities for the students to use their talents and gifts.
  • To uplift the intellectual, social and physical needs of all students
  • To bring the school and community closer to each other.
  • To provide good moral education.
  • To impart social and spiritual values and good ideals to the students.
  • To provide technical and Higher Education to all the pupils.
