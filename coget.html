CHURCH OF GOD EDUCATIONAL TRUST

The Church of God Educational Trust (COGET) was established in 1975 with the aim of providing quality education. They look after all the Church of God Schools, private and Government aided, in Meghalaya and few schools in Assam.

ABOUT COGET
  • Looking after all Church of God Schools private and Government aided schools in Meghalaya and few schools in Assam
  • There are 124 schools in Meghalaya and some parts in Assam.
  • There are 100 teachers non-Government aided.
  • COGET was registered under Trust in the year 2010- There are 17 members in this committee.
  • They conduct General Body meeting from time to time.
  • They plan visitation to all schools when required.
  • They conduct interviews and appoint new teachers.
